The Vacation of My Dreams

Located on the southern tip of the Balkan Peninsula, offering more coastline than any other country on the Mediterranean Basin, and boasting the birthplace of Western civilization, the place I've always dreamed of visiting is:

Greece!

Greece is one of the most visited countries in Europe, and the entire world, for a variety of reasons:

  • Beautiful, ancient, and historic architecture
  • Rich culture and history
  • Thousands of explorable islands
  • Beautiful landscapes and scenery
  • Delectable cuisine

Architecture

“The center of Western culture is Greece, and we have never lost our ties with the architectural concepts of that ancient civilization.”

Stephen Gardiner

Architecure in Greece dates back several millenia. Some Greek buildings were built as long ago as 600 BC! Some of these building include:

  • The Parthenon
  • Temple of Apollo
  • The Erechtheion
  • The Temple of Zeus
  • The Theatre of Dionysus

Culture and History

"We are all Greeks. Our laws, our literature, our religion, our arts have their root in Greece."

Percy Bysshe Shelley

Greece is commonly referred to as the cradle of Western civilization, and for good reason! The Greeks made numerous contributions to areas of study such as:

  • Philosophy
  • Mathematics
  • Astronomy
  • Medicine
  • Political Science

Greek Islands

“In every island of the Aegean Sea are found abundant traces of a vast prehistoric empire.”

James Theodore Bent

Greece has many islands, and some estimate that there are as many as 6,000! Some popular tourist destinations include:

  • Crete
  • Santorini
  • Mykonos
  • Rhodes
  • Corfu

Scenery

"The sea was sapphire coloured, and the sky
Burned like a heated opal through the air;
We hoisted sail; the wind was blowing fair
For the blue lands that to the eastward lie."

Oscar Wilde

Greece boasts some of the most beautiful places to visit on Earth:

  • Oia Village, Santorini Island
  • Athens Acropolis
  • Meteora Monasteries
  • Chania, Crete Island
  • Village of Naoussa, Paros Island

Greek cuisine

"Part of why I love Greek food so much is that it is simple, but it's unpretentious. It's authentic."

Priness Tatiana of Greece and Denmark

Outside of its rich culture and history and natural beauty, Greek food is world famous! Popular Greek dishes include:

  • Moussaka
  • Saganaki
  • Souvlaki
  • Dolmades
  • Spanakopita
